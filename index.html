About Me

I am Galana Fekadu Asafa, a passionate computer scientist with a strong foundation in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. I earned my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Technology from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (Chengdu, China), and continued to deepen my expertise with a Master of Engineering in Computer Science and Technology from Central South University (Changsha, China). With experience in designing innovative algorithms, developing deep learning models, and solving complex real-world problems, I am committed to leveraging technology to create impactful solutions. My work blends rigorous research with practical applications, driven by curiosity, creativity, and a dedication to continuous learning. I enjoy exploring the frontiers of AI, experimenting with new technologies, and contributing to projects that bridge research and industry.
